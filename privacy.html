Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, display name, and profile picture URL. That's the entire personal-data set we hold for you.

2. What we do with it

  • Identify you on return visits so your plan and history are restored.
  • Send you the rare service email if your account requires action.
  • Aggregate, anonymised usage counts to improve the product.

3. What we never do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties.
  • We do not read, store, or transmit the contents of any PDF, Word, or image file you process. Files are deleted from disk the moment your request finishes.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Cookies / local storage

We store a small JSON blob in your browser's localStorage called hope.user that holds your verified profile so you don't have to sign in on every visit. Clear it any time from your browser settings.

5. Files on the server

Uploaded files are written to a temporary directory, processed, returned to you, and deleted in the response close handler. We do not back them up, archive them, or read them after the response.

6. Contact

Questions? Email hopepdfofficial@gmail.com.
